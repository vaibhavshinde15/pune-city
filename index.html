QUEEN OF DECCAN: PUNE CITY

               

Pune, often called the "Oxford of the East," is a vibrant city in Maharashtra, India, known for its rich history, educational institutions, and IT sector, and is the second largest city in the state.

  • Location and Significance:

    • Pune is located on the Deccan Plateau in the state of Maharashtra. It's a key political and cultural center, and a major hub for education and IT.

  • Nicknames:

    • Pune is known as the "Oxford of the East" due to its numerous educational institutions and the high number of colleges.

  • Historical Significance:

    • Pune has a rich history, with the Shaniwar Wada, a historical fortification, being a notable landmark representing the city's Maratha heritage.   More Details

  • Economy:

    • Pune is a major IT center and has a vibrant economy, with a highly educated workforce.   More Details

  • Tourism:
